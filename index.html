About

Blockout 2024 is an online movement sparked by a viral TikTok video criticizing celebrity extravagance at the Met Gala amid the genocide being committed against Palestinians in Gaza by Israeli forces, according to respected international institutions and legal experts. The movement encourages blocking insensitive celebrities and organizations on social media over their perceived indifference to the catastrophic human rights violations in Gaza.
